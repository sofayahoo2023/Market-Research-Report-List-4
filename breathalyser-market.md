<p><p>Breathalyzers are devices used to measure a person's blood alcohol content from a breath sample. The market for breathalyzers can be divided into two main types: desktop and portable. Desktop breathalyzers are larger and stay in a fixed location, such as at a police station or in a workplace setting. Portable breathalyzers are smaller and can be carried around for on-the-go testing, such as by law enforcement officers or individuals monitoring their own alcohol consumption. Both types serve the same purpose of promoting safety and preventing drunk driving.</p></p>
