<p><p>Warehouse Management System (WMS) market includes two main types: Standalone Systems and Integrated Systems. Standalone Systems are individual systems that are commonly used by smaller businesses to manage their warehouse operations independently. Integrated Systems, on the other hand, are part of larger enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems and are used by bigger organizations to streamline and automate their entire supply chain process. Both types cater to different business sizes and requirements, offering various features and functions to optimize warehouse operations.</p></p>
