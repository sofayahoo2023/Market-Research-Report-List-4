<p><p>Cardioverter Defibrillator Devices market consists of two main types - Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD) and External Cardioverter Defibrillator. ICDs are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor heart rhythm and deliver a shock to restore normal rhythm if needed. External Cardioverter Defibrillators are portable devices used in emergency situations to deliver a shock to the heart. Both types play a crucial role in treating life-threatening arrhythmias and preventing sudden cardiac death.</p></p>
