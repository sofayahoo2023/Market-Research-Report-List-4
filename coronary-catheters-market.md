<p><p>Coronary catheters are medical devices used in various cardiac procedures. Aspiration catheters are used to remove blood clots from coronary arteries, while coronary balloon catheters are used to inflate and widen narrowed or blocked arteries. Diagnostic catheters help in assessing the condition of arteries, and guide catheters assist in navigating and placing other catheters within the coronary arteries. These devices play a crucial role in treating heart conditions and improving blood flow to the heart muscle.</p></p>
