<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Stripping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripping</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor photoresist stripping market can be categorized into two types: positive photoresist stripping and negative photoresist stripping. Positive photoresist stripping involves removing the unexposed areas of the photoresist material, leaving behind the patterned areas for further processing. On the other hand, negative photoresist stripping removes the exposed areas of the photoresist material, creating patterns in the unexposed areas. Both methods are essential in semiconductor manufacturing to accurately transfer patterns onto the semiconductor wafer for various applications.</p></p>
