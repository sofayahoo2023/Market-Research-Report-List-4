<p><p>Stainless steel conveyor belts are available in two main types: straight line and curve. Straight line conveyor belts are used for moving products in a straight path from one point to another, making them suitable for applications where products need to be transported efficiently and quickly. Curve conveyor belts, on the other hand, are designed to transport products around corners and bends, providing flexibility and enabling the conveyor system to navigate through more complex layouts and spaces.</p></p>
