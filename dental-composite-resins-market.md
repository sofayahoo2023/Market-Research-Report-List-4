<p><p>Dental composite resins are classified based on the size of the fillers used. Macrofilled fillers are larger-sized particles, providing strength and durability. Microfilled fillers are smaller particles, offering improved aesthetics. Hybrid fillers combine the benefits of both macrofilled and microfilled resins. Nanofilled fillers contain extremely small particles for exceptional polishability and wear resistance. Bulk fillers are designed for fast and efficient filling of larger cavities. Each type offers unique advantages for various dental applications.</p></p>
