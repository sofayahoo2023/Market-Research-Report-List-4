They operate continuously and provide a stable output power. On the other hand, Pulse Klystrons are designed for applications that require high peak powers in short pulses, such as in military radar systems and medical imaging equipment. They deliver pulsed RF signals with precisely controlled timing and amplitude. Both types of klystrons play important roles in various industries that rely on high-power microwave technology.</p></p>
