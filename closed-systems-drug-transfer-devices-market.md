<p><p>Closed Systems Drug Transfer Devices market consists of various types, including Membrane-to-Membrane Systems and Needleless Systems. Membrane-to-Membrane Systems utilize a barrier membrane to prevent the escape of hazardous drugs during transfer, ensuring the safety of healthcare workers. Needleless Systems, on the other hand, eliminate the need for needles during drug transfer, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries and contamination. Both types of systems are designed to enhance the safety and efficiency of drug transfer processes in healthcare settings.</p></p>
