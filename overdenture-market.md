<p><p>Overdenture market consists of three main types: Simple Overdenture, Root Cap Overdenture, and Telescopic Crown Overdenture. Simple Overdenture involves placing complete dentures over natural teeth or dental implants. Root Cap Overdenture involves using natural roots as anchors for the denture. Telescopic Crown Overdenture involves using a combination of primary and secondary crowns for better stability and retention. These different types cater to varying degrees of tooth loss and provide options for patients seeking a more secure and comfortable denture solution.</p></p>
