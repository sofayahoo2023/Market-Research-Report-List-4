<p><p>Geothermal Heat Pumps (GHPs) have different market types based on their installation methods. Vertical Closed Loop systems use vertical wells drilled deep into the ground. Horizontal Closed Loop systems have loops buried horizontally underground. Open Loop systems use groundwater directly from a well or surface body of water. These different market types cater to a variety of installation needs, providing flexibility for different environments and requirements. Each type offers unique advantages and considerations for efficient heating and cooling.</p></p>
