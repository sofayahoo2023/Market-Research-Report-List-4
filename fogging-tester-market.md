<p><strong>The Fogging Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gloss Method</li><li>Gravimetric Method</li><li>Haze Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fogging Tester market includes three main types: Gloss Method, Gravimetric Method, and Haze Method. The Gloss Method measures the amount of fog generated on a surface by comparing gloss before and after fog exposure. The Gravimetric Method measures the weight of residue deposited on a surface after fog exposure. The Haze Method measures the amount of light scattered by the fogged surface. Each method provides valuable information for assessing the fogging characteristics of materials and products in various industries.</p></p>
