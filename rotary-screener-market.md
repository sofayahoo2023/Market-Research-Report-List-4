<p><p>A rotary screener, also known as a rotary sieve or rotary sifter, is a device that is used to separate and classify materials based on particle size. It consists of a cylindrical drum that is rotated to impart motion to the material being screened. As the material moves along the drum, particles that are smaller than the sieve openings fall through, while larger particles are retained and discharged at the end of the drum.</p><p>The global market for rotary screeners is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective screening solutions across various industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food processing is driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in technology and automation in screening equipment are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising emphasis on quality control and product safety in manufacturing processes is leading to the adoption of rotary screeners to ensure the uniformity and purity of the final product.
