<p><p>Pneumatic water pumps are categorized into two main types: submersible and dry pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in the water they are pumping, making them ideal for applications such as wells and tanks. Dry pumps, on the other hand, are designed to operate outside of the water source and are commonly used for dewatering applications. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industrial, commercial, and residential settings for various water pumping needs.</p></p>
