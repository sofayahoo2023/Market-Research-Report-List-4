<p><strong>The Rotary Tiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-tine</li><li>Rear-tine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary tillers come in two main types: front-tine and rear-tine. Front-tine tillers are designed with tines at the front of the machine, making it easier to maneuver in tight spaces and around obstacles. These tillers are typically lighter and more affordable, making them a popular choice for small to medium-sized gardens. On the other hand, rear-tine tillers have tines located at the back of the machine, providing more power and stability for tough soil conditions. They are ideal for larger gardens or farms that require more heavy-duty tilling.</p></p>
