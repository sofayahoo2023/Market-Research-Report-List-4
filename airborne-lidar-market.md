<p><p>Airborne LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) is a remote sensing technology used to collect high-resolution elevation data of the Earth's surface. It involves mounting LiDAR sensors on aircraft to capture 3D data points, which are then processed to create detailed maps of terrain, vegetation, buildings, and other features.</p><p>The Airborne LiDAR market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for accurate and detailed geospatial data in various industries such as surveying, urban planning, forestry, and infrastructure development. The technology offers advantages over traditional methods like photogrammetry and ground surveys, as it can capture data quickly and efficiently over large areas, even in challenging terrains.</p><p>One of the key trends driving the market growth is the adoption of advanced LiDAR sensors with improved accuracy, range, and data processing capabilities. Furthermore, the integration of LiDAR with other technologies like GPS and cameras is enabling more comprehensive data collection for diverse applications.</p><p>Overall, the Airborne LiDAR Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period, as organizations increasingly recognize the value of high-quality geospatial data for decision-making and planning purposes.</p></p>
