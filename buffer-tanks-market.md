<p><ul><li>Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT)</li><li>Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buffer tanks are widely used in HVAC systems to store excess hot or chilled water to maintain system efficiency. Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT) are used in chilled water systems to prevent short cycling of chillers and provide thermal energy storage. Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT) are used in hot water systems to store excess heat and prevent rapid boiler cycling. Both types of buffer tanks help reduce energy consumption and increase the lifespan of HVAC equipment by ensuring stable system operation.</p></p>
