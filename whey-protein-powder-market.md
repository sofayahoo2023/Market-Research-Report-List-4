<p><strong>The Whey Protein Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Concentrate Powder</li><li>Whey Protein Isolate Powder</li><li>Whey Protein Hydrolysate Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Whey protein powder is available in three main types - whey protein concentrate powder, whey protein isolate powder, and whey protein hydrolysate powder. Whey protein concentrate powder contains around 70-80% protein and includes some fats and carbohydrates. Whey protein isolate powder undergoes further processing to remove more fats and carbohydrates, resulting in a product that is around 90% protein. Whey protein hydrolysate powder is partially predigested, making it easier to digest and absorb quickly. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
