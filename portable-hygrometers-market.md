<p><p>Portable hygrometers come in two main types: single channel and dual channel. Single channel hygrometers are designed to measure humidity in one location, making them ideal for simple applications. On the other hand, dual channel hygrometers can measure humidity in two different locations simultaneously, providing more versatile and accurate data for complex environments. Both types of hygrometers are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and HVAC to monitor and control humidity levels for optimal performance.</p></p>
