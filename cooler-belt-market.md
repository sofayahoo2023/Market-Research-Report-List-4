<p><p>Cooler belt market consists of two main types: rubber belts and leather belts. Rubber belts are more commonly used in cooling systems due to their durability, flexibility, and resistance to temperature fluctuations. They are ideal for heavy-duty applications and provide excellent grip for transporting materials through the cooling process. On the other hand, leather belts are known for their strength and smooth surface, which makes them suitable for lighter loads and applications requiring a more gentle touch. Both types of belts play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency of cooling systems across various industries.</p></p>
