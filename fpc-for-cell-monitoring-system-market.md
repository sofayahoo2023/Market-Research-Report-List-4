<p><p>FPC (Flexible Printed Circuits) for Cell Monitoring System are available in three market types: Single-Side FPC, Double-Sided FPC, and Multi-Layer FPC. Single-Side FPC consists of a single layer of flexible circuitry, making it lightweight and cost-effective. Double-Sided FPC has circuitry on both sides of the flexible substrate, providing more design flexibility. Multi-Layer FPC includes multiple layers of circuitry stacked together, allowing for complex electronic functions in a compact form factor. Each type offers unique benefits for cell monitoring system applications.</p></p>
