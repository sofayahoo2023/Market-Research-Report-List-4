<p><p>Artificial Vascular Implants come in various types, including Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene (EPTFE), Polyethylene Terephthalate, Polyurethane, and others. Polyurethane implants are known for their softness and ability to mimic natural blood vessels. Other types of implants may include silicone or composite materials, each offering unique advantages in terms of biocompatibility, durability, and flexibility.</p></p>
