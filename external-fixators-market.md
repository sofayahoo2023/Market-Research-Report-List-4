<p><p>External fixators are surgical devices used to stabilize broken bones and promote proper healing. They are often used in cases of severe fractures, open fractures, or bone infections. External fixators work by attaching metal pins or screws to the affected bone, which are then connected to an external frame to hold the bone in place.</p><p>The External Fixators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period. The key driving factors for this growth include an increasing incidence of bone fractures due to a growing geriatric population, rising prevalence of osteoporosis, and rising number of road accidents. Additionally, advancements in technology leading to the development of more efficient external fixators are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the external fixators market is the development of hybrid fixators, which combine the advantages of both external and internal fixators. These hybrid fixators provide more stability and better cosmetic outcomes for patients. Another trend is the increasing adoption of minimally invasive external fixators, which reduce the risk of infection and speed up the healing process. Overall, the external fixators market is expected to continue to grow as the demand for orthopedic surgical procedures increases.</p></p>
