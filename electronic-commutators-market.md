<p><ul><li>Groove Commutator</li><li>Hook Type Commutator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic commutators are used in electric motors to control the direction of current flow. There are two main types of commutators: groove commutators and hook type commutators. Groove commutators have slotted grooves where the brushes make contact, while hook type commutators have metal hook-like segments that the brushes glide over. Both types are essential for efficient motor operation and can be found in various applications such as appliances, power tools, and automotive systems. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific motor requirements.</p></p>
