Forceps and spatulas are used for grasping tissues, while retractors are used to hold back tissues during surgery. Dilators are utilized to expand or stretch an opening in the body. Graspers are designed to hold and manipulate tissues during procedures. Auxiliary instruments assist in facilitating surgical procedures, while cutter instruments are used for cutting or dissecting tissues during surgery.</p></p>
