<p><p>Mining thickeners are used to separate solids from liquids in the mining industry. There are two main types of mining thickeners based on their drive mechanism: peripheral drive and center drive. Peripheral drive thickeners have a rotating mechanism on the outside of the tank, while center drive thickeners have a rotating mechanism in the center of the tank. Peripheral drive thickeners are known for their efficient operation and lower maintenance costs, while center drive thickeners are preferred for their stability and reliability.</p></p>
