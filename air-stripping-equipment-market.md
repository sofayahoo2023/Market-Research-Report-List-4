<p><strong>The Air Stripping Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sieve Tray System</li><li>Packed Tower System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air stripping equipment market comprises two main types: Sieve Tray System and Packed Tower System. The Sieve Tray System utilizes perforated trays to facilitate the transfer of contaminants from water to air through a series of stages. On the other hand, the Packed Tower System relies on packing material such as structured packing or random packing to increase surface area for mass transfer. Both systems are effective in removing volatile organic compounds from water sources, with each having its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
