<p><p>Electronic airlaid paper market is segmented into two types: chemical bonding and thermal bonding. Chemical bonding involves using adhesives or chemicals to bond the fibers in the paper together, resulting in a strong and durable material. Thermal bonding, on the other hand, uses heat to melt synthetic fibers within the paper, creating a secure bond. Both types of bonding techniques are used in the production of electronic airlaid paper for various applications in industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
