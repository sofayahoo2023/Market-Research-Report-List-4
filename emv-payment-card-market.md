<p><p>The EMV Payment Card market is divided into three main types: Contactless Card, Contact Card, and Dual Interface Card. Contactless cards allow for payment by simply tapping the card on a terminal, without the need for physical contact. Contact cards require the card to be inserted into a terminal for payment. Dual Interface cards combine both contact and contactless capabilities, providing users with more flexibility in how they make payments. These different types cater to various preferences and needs of consumers in the market.</p></p>
