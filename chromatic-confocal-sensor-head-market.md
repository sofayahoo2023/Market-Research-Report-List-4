<p><p>Chromatic confocal sensor heads are used in various markets such as displacement sensor heads and thickness sensor heads. Displacement sensor heads measure the distance between the sensor head and the target object, providing accurate positional information. Thickness sensor heads measure the thickness of an object by capturing the distance between the sensor head and the surfaces of the object. Both types of sensor heads are used in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace for precise measurement and inspection applications.</p></p>
