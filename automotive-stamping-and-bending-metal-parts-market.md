<p><p>Automotive stamping and bending metal parts market involves various processes such as blanking, embossing, bending, coining, and flanging. Blanking is the cutting of a flat shape from sheet metal. Embossing creates raised or sunken designs on the metal surface. Bending involves shaping the metal by force. Coining is a precision stamping process for producing coins or medals. Flanging is the bending or forming of a protruding edge on a metal part. These processes are crucial in the manufacturing of automotive components.</p></p>
