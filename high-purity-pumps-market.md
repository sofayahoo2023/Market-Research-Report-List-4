<p><p>High purity pumps are classified into different types based on their mechanism of operation. Bellows pumps use a flexible tube to move fluids, ensuring high purity by minimizing contact with external surfaces. Diaphragm pumps use a diaphragm to pump fluids, preventing contamination. Metering pumps accurately control the flow rate of fluids, making them suitable for high purity applications. Other types of high purity pumps include centrifugal pumps, gear pumps, and peristaltic pumps, each offering unique advantages for maintaining purity in critical processes.</p></p>
