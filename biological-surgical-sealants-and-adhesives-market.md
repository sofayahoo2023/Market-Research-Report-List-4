<p><p>Biological surgical sealants and adhesives are types of products used in surgical procedures to promote tissue healing and seal wounds. There are three main types of biological surgical sealants and adhesives: fibrin-based, collagen-based, and albumin-based. Fibrin-based products utilize fibrinogen and thrombin to form a clot and adhere tissue together. Collagen-based products use collagen fibers to provide strength and promote tissue regeneration. Albumin-based products use albumin proteins to seal wounds and promote healing. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different surgical applications.</p></p>
