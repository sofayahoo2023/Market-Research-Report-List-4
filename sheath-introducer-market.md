<p><p>Sheath introducers are medical devices used in procedures such as catheterization. There are two main types of sheath introducers: straight sheaths and curved sheaths. Straight sheaths are designed in a straight shape for easy insertion and placement, while curved sheaths are designed with a curve to facilitate navigation through narrower or more complex anatomical structures.
