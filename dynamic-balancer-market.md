<p><p>Dynamic balancers come in three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic balancers require some manual input but still offer a high level of automation for increased accuracy and speed. Manual balancers, on the other hand, rely entirely on human operation and are typically used for simpler balancing tasks. Each type of balancer offers unique benefits and features to cater to different balancing needs and preferences.</p></p>
