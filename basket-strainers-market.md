<p><p>Basket strainers are devices used to filter out unwanted particles from liquid flow systems. There are two main types of basket strainers on the market: simplex and duplex. Simplex basket strainers are designed for applications where the flow can be shut off for maintenance, while duplex basket strainers consist of two separate strainer baskets that allow for continuous operation as one basket can be cleaned while the other is still in use. Both types are important for ensuring the efficiency and reliability of liquid flow systems.</p></p>
