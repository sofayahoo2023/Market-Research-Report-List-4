There are four main types of anti-icing systems on the market: Electric Pulse Anti-Icing Systems, Liquid Anti-Icing Systems, Hot Air Anti-Icing Systems, and Electric Heating Anti-Icing Systems. Electric Pulse systems use electromagnetic pulses to prevent ice formation, while Liquid systems apply de-icing fluid to surfaces. Hot Air systems blow warm air onto aircraft surfaces, and Electric Heating systems use electrical heating elements to melt ice.
