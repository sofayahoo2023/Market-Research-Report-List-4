<p><strong>The Night Vision Monocular Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night vision monoculars are available in two main types: Image intensifier and thermal image. Image intensifier night vision monoculars amplify ambient light to provide a clear image in low-light conditions. They are ideal for use in urban environments or areas with some light sources. Thermal image night vision monoculars, on the other hand, detect heat signatures to produce an image, allowing users to see in complete darkness. They are commonly used by military and law enforcement personnel for surveillance and security purposes.</p></p>
