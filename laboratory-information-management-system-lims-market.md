<p><p>A Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is a software system used to manage samples, data, and workflows within a laboratory. The market for LIMS can be divided into two main types: On-premise LIMS and Cloud-based LIMS. On-premise LIMS require the software to be installed and run on local servers, providing more control over data security but requiring higher upfront costs. Cloud-based LIMS, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers, offering greater accessibility and scalability at a lower initial investment.</p></p>
