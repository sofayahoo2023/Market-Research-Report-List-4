<p><p>Butter concentrate market can be categorized into two main types: solid butter concentrate and liquid butter concentrate. Solid butter concentrate is typically made by removing the milk solids and water from butter, resulting in a concentrated form that is solid at room temperature. On the other hand, liquid butter concentrate is created by removing most of the water content from butter, leaving behind a concentrated liquid form. Both types of butter concentrate are used by food manufacturers to enhance flavor and texture in various products.</p></p>
