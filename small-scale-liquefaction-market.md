<p><p>Small-scale liquefaction market can be categorized into two types based on the process used: direct liquefaction and indirect liquefaction. In direct liquefaction, natural gas is converted into liquid form through a process involving cooling and compression. On the other hand, indirect liquefaction involves converting natural gas into a gas form like hydrogen or syngas first, followed by conversion into liquid form. Both processes have their own advantages and applications in small-scale liquefaction markets.</p></p>
