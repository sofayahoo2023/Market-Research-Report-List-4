<p><ul><li>Horizontally Mounted</li><li>Vertically Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mud agitators are used in drilling operations to maintain the consistency of drilling mud. There are two main types of mud agitators based on their mounting position: horizontally mounted and vertically mounted. </p><p>Horizontally mounted mud agitators are typically used in tanks with limited headroom, as they are placed directly on the top of the mud tank. Vertically mounted mud agitators, on the other hand, are mounted on the side of the tank and are ideal for taller tanks where space is not limited. Each type of mud agitator caters to different tank configurations and operating conditions.</p></p>
