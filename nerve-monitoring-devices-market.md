There are different types of nerve monitoring devices: EEG (electroencephalography) for measuring brain activity, evoked potential for detecting the response to stimuli, EMG (electromyography) for evaluating muscle activity, and ECoG (electrocorticography) for monitoring brain activity directly from the cerebral cortex.
