<p><p>Vaccines for fish aquaculture are available in three main types: Oral vaccines, Injectable vaccines, and Other vaccines. Oral vaccines are administered through the fish's food, making them easy to deliver to large populations. Injectable vaccines are injected into the fish, providing direct immunity. Other vaccines include immersion vaccines, which are added to the water for fish to absorb through their gills. Each type of vaccine offers different benefits and may be used depending on the specific needs of the aquaculture operation.</p></p>
