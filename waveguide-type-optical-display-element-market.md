<p><p>Waveguide-type optical display elements consist of holographic waveguides and reflective waveguides in the market. Holographic waveguides use diffraction grating to direct light towards the viewer's eyes, creating vibrant and immersive holographic images. Reflective waveguides, on the other hand, use mirrors to bounce light back and forth within the waveguide, producing high-resolution and bright displays. Both types of waveguides are gaining popularity in augmented reality and mixed reality applications due to their compact design and ability to provide clear and realistic visuals.</p></p>
