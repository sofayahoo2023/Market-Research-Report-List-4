<p><p>Laparotomy sponges are used in surgical procedures to control bleeding and absorb fluids. There are several types of laparotomy sponges available in the market. Radiopaque laparotomy sponges are designed with an X-ray detectable strip for easy identification. Traditional laparotomy sponges are made of soft, absorbent material. RFID or barcode laparotomy sponges have tracking technology embedded in them for inventory management and to prevent retained sponges. Each type offers unique benefits for use in surgical settings.</p></p>
