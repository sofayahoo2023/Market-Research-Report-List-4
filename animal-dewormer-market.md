<p><p>Animal dewormer markets can be categorized based on their method of application, either external or internal use. External use dewormers are applied directly onto the animal's skin or fur to combat external parasites like fleas and ticks. On the other hand, internal use dewormers are administered orally or through injections to eliminate internal parasites like worms and larvae. Both types of dewormers are essential for maintaining the health and well-being of animals by protecting them from harmful parasitic infections.</p></p>
