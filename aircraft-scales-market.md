<p><ul><li>Platform</li><li>Floor-standing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platform aircraft scales are designed for smaller aircraft and helicopters, providing a portable and easy-to-use solution for weight measurement. These scales are typically placed directly under the wheels of the aircraft for accurate readings. Floor-standing aircraft scales are larger and more robust, suitable for weighing larger commercial aircraft. They are often permanently installed in hangars or on runways for frequent and consistent weighing. Both types of scales play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of aircraft operations.</p></p>
