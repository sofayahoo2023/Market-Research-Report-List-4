<p><ul><li>Visual Type Lensmeter</li><li>Projection Type Lensmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visual Type Lensmeter measures the lens power by visually aligning the target with a graduated scale, making it suitable for quick and basic measurements. Projection Type Lensmeter, on the other hand, uses a projection screen to display the lens power, providing more accurate and efficient measurements. Both types of lensmeters are used in optometry practices for determining the prescription of eyeglasses and contact lenses. The choice between visual and projection type lensmeter depends on the level of precision and speed required for the measurements.</p></p>
