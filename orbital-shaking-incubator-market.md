<p><p>Orbital shaking incubators come in two main types: closed type and open type. Closed type incubators provide a controlled environment with limited air exchange, ideal for applications requiring precise temperature and humidity control. Open type incubators, on the other hand, allow for faster equilibration and are more suitable for applications where frequent access to samples is required. Both types cater to specific research needs and are used in various industries such as biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and research laboratories.</p></p>
