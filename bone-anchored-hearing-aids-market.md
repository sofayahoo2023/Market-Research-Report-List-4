<p><p>Bone Anchored Hearing Aids market can be classified into two types: implantable and non-implantable. Implantable devices involve a surgical procedure to anchor the device to the skull bone, providing a more stable and permanent solution for hearing loss. On the other hand, non-implantable devices are worn on the head or behind the ear, offering a less invasive and temporary solution. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of individuals with hearing impairments.</p></p>
