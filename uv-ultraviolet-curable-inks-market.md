<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The UV (Ultraviolet) Curable Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Radical Polymerization Type</li><li>Free Radical Addition Molding</li><li>Cationic Polymerization</li><li>Acid Cure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV (Ultraviolet) Curable Inks are categorized into different types based on their curing mechanisms. Free Radical Polymerization Type involves the use of initiators to polymerize the ink upon exposure to UV light. Free Radical Addition Molding utilizes monomers that undergo addition polymerization under UV light. Cationic Polymerization involves the use of cationic initiators to cure the ink. Acid Cure Type relies on acid catalysts to crosslink the ink components upon UV exposure. Each type offers specific benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
