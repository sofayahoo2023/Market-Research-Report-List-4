<p><p>Offshore Drilling Rigs market includes three main types: Jackup Offshore Drilling Rigs, Semi-Submersibles Offshore Drilling Rigs, and Drillships. Jackup rigs are mobile and rest on the sea floor while drilling takes place, suitable for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are partially submerged underwater and are used in deeper waters. Drillships are ships equipped with drilling equipment and can operate in ultra-deep waters. Each type caters to different water depths and drilling requirements in the offshore drilling industry.</p></p>
