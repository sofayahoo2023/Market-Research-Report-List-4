<p><p>Acrylic sheets are commonly found in two main market types: cast acrylic sheet and extruded acrylic sheet. Cast acrylic sheet is produced using a casting process, creating a higher quality product with better optical clarity and surface finish. Both types of acrylic sheets offer a wide range of applications, including signage, displays, and protective barriers.</p></p>
