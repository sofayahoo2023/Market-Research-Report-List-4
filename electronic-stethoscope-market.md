<p><p>The Electronic Stethoscope market is divided into two main types: Amplifying Stethoscopes and Digitizing Stethoscopes. Amplifying stethoscopes have a built-in amplifier that enhances the sound of the heartbeat or other bodily sounds, making it easier for healthcare professionals to hear and diagnose. On the other hand, Digitizing stethoscopes convert the sound into digital signals, allowing for further analysis and storage of the data for future reference. Both types offer advanced features that improve the accuracy and efficiency of diagnosing patients.</p></p>
