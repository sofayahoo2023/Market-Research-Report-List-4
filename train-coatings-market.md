<p><p>Train coatings are divided into two main types: primer and top coat. Primer coatings are applied as the first layer to improve adhesion, corrosion resistance, and durability of the surface. They serve as a base for the top coat, which provides color, gloss, and additional protection against weathering and abrasion. Both primer and top coat can be customized based on specific requirements such as UV resistance, chemical resistance, and aesthetic appeal, making them essential components of the train coatings market.</p></p>
