<p><p>Smart glass for automotive market includes three main types: electrochromic, SPD (suspended particle device), and PDLC (polymer dispersed liquid crystal). Electrochromic glass can change its tint or opacity when a voltage is applied, providing control over light and heat. SPD technology uses suspended particles to control the transparency of the glass, while PDLC uses liquid crystals dispersed in a polymer matrix for the same purpose. These technologies offer benefits such as energy efficiency, glare reduction, and privacy control in automotive applications.</p></p>
