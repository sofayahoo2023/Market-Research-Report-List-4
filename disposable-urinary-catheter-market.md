<p><p>Disposable urinary catheter market types include Foley catheters, intermittent catheters, and external catheters. Foley catheters are typically left in place for a longer period of time and are held in the bladder by a small balloon. Intermittent catheters are used periodically to empty the bladder and are removed after each use. External catheters are worn externally and may be used by male patients who have difficulty with traditional catheter insertion. Each type of catheter serves a specific purpose in managing urinary issues.</p></p>
