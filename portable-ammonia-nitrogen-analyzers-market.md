<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable ammonia nitrogen analyzers can be categorized into two main types: colorimetric method and electrode method. The colorimetric method involves the use of reagents that change color in the presence of ammonia nitrogen, which can then be measured visually or with a spectrophotometer. On the other hand, the electrode method utilizes an ammonia ion-selective electrode to directly measure the concentration of ammonia nitrogen in a sample. Both methods offer portable and convenient solutions for on-site ammonia nitrogen analysis in various industries.</p></p>
