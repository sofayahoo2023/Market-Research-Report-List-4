<p><p>Dental burs are rotary cutting tools used by dentists to shape and remove material from teeth and bones in various dental procedures. There are three main types of dental burs based on their shank design: Long Straight Shank (HP), Latch-type Shank (RA), and Friction Grip Shank (FG). HP burs have a long straight shank used with high-speed handpieces, RA burs have a latch-type shank used with low-speed handpieces, and FG burs have a friction grip shank used with high-speed handpieces for better stability and precision during dental procedures.</p></p>
