<p><p>Ink dispersants are used in the ink industry to disperse pigment particles evenly in the ink formulation. There are two main types of ink dispersants: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based dispersants are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, making them a popular choice for many ink manufacturers. On the other hand, solvent-based dispersants offer higher performance in terms of dispersion stability and ink quality. Both types of dispersants play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and quality of ink production processes.</p></p>
