They are commonly used for patients who need large volumes of fluid quickly. Micro Drip sets deliver smaller drops, usually around 60 drops per milliliter, and are used for precise and slow administration of fluids. Each type of drip set is designed to meet specific patient needs and medical requirements.</p></p>
