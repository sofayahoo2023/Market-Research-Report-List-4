<p><ul><li>Zinc finger nucleases (ZFNs)</li><li>TALENs</li><li>CRISPR-Cas system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene editing tools market can be categorized into Zinc finger nucleases (ZFNs), TALENs, and CRISPR-Cas system. ZFNs and TALENs are older technologies that use engineered proteins to target specific DNA sequences for editing. On the other hand, CRISPR-Cas system is a newer and more efficient tool that uses RNA to guide the Cas protein to the desired DNA sequence for editing. Each of these tools has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in gene editing.</p></p>
