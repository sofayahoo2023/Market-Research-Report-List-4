<p><strong>The Industrial Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical roller bearings (SRB)</li><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing (DGBB)</li><li>Cylindrical roller bearings (CRB)</li><li>Double-row Tapered Roller Bearing (DTRB)</li><li>Spherical Thrust Roller Bearing (STRB)</li><li>Angular contact ball bearings (ACBB)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial bearing market comprises various types of bearings catering to different applications in industries. Spherical roller bearings (SRB) are designed to handle high radial loads, while Deep Groove Ball Bearings (DGBB) are suitable for moderate radial and axial loads. Cylindrical roller bearings (CRB) excel in high radial load applications, whereas Double-row Tapered Roller Bearings (DTRB) offer both radial and axial load support. Spherical Thrust Roller Bearings (STRB) are ideal for high thrust loads, Angular contact ball bearings (ACBB) are designed for combined radial and axial loads, and others cater to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
