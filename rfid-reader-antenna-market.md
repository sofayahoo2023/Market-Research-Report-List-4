<p><p>RFID Reader Antenna market consists of two main types: Directional and Omnidirectional antennas. Directional RFID Reader Antennas are designed to emit a focused signal in a specific direction, allowing for more precise reading of tags in a particular area. Omnidirectional RFID Reader Antennas, on the other hand, emit a 360-degree signal, enabling them to read tags from all directions. Both types cater to different industry needs, with directional antennas offering pinpoint accuracy and omnidirectional antennas providing broader coverage.</p></p>
