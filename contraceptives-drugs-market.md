<p><p>Contraceptives drugs market can be categorized into three types: long-acting contraceptives, short-acting contraceptives, and emergency contraceptives. Long-acting contraceptives, such as intrauterine devices and implants, provide protection against pregnancy for an extended period of time. Short-acting contraceptives, like pills and patches, require regular use to prevent pregnancy. Emergency contraceptives, often referred to as the "morning after pill," are meant to be used after unprotected sex to reduce the risk of pregnancy. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences of individuals seeking contraception.</p></p>
