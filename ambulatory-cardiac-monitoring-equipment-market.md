<p><ul><li>Holter Monitor</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment includes various types such as Holter monitors, event monitors, and mobile cardiac telemetry. Holter monitors are portable devices that continuously record heart rhythms for 24-48 hours. Event monitors are used to record heart activity during specific symptoms. Mobile cardiac telemetry devices are worn by the patient for extended monitoring periods and transmit data to healthcare providers remotely. Other types of ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment may include implantable loop recorders or wearable patch monitors.</p></p>
