MBR systems use membranes to separate solid and liquid components, CMF systems utilize ceramic membranes for filtration, SMF systems employ spiral membranes for purification, and TWF systems utilize tubular membranes for water treatment. Each of these technologies offers unique benefits and applications in the field of water treatment and purification.</p></p>
