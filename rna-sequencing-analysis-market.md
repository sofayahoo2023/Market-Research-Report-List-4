<p><p>RNA sequencing analysis involves studying different types of RNA molecules present in a sample. Total RNA sequencing provides a holistic view of all RNA molecules in a cell, while pre-mRNA sequencing specifically focuses on precursor messenger RNA molecules before they are processed into mature mRNA. Noncoding RNA sequencing is dedicated to studying RNA molecules that do not code for proteins but play important regulatory roles in gene expression. Each type of RNA sequencing analysis offers unique insights into the functioning of cells and organisms.</p></p>
