<p><p>Optical communication equipments refer to devices used in transmitting data through optical signals. These equipments are categorized into three main types: Modulator/Demodulator, Transmitter/Receiver, and Optical Fiber market. Modulator/Demodulator devices are responsible for encoding and decoding data into optical signals. Transmitter/Receiver devices are used for transmitting and receiving these signals. The Optical Fiber market refers to the market for the actual optic cables that carry the data between devices. Together, these equipments form the backbone of optical communication networks.</p></p>
