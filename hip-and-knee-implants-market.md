<p><ul><li>Primary Implant</li><li>Partial or Unicompartmental Implant</li><li>Revision Implant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Primary implants are used for initial joint replacement surgeries, replacing damaged or diseased hip or knee joints with prosthetic components. Partial or unicompartmental implants are used when only one compartment of the joint is affected, providing a more conservative treatment option. Revision implants are used for patients who have already undergone joint replacement but require a second surgery due to complications or wear and tear of the original implant. These different types of implants cater to varying needs and conditions of patients requiring joint replacement surgery.</p></p>
