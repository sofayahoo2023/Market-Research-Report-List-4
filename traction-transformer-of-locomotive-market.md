<p><p>Traction transformers are key components in locomotive systems, converting high voltage electrical power from the grid into suitable levels for the locomotive's traction motors. Two main types are used in the market: Alternative Current (AC) systems and Direct Current (DC) systems. AC systems are more commonly used in modern locomotives due to their efficiency and ability to transmit power over longer distances. DC systems, although older technology, are still utilized in some locomotives for their simplicity and ease of control.</p></p>
