<p><p>A rail-mounted inspection robot is a type of robot that is designed to move along railway tracks and inspect various components of the track infrastructure such as rails, sleepers, and connectors. These robots are typically equipped with sensors and cameras to detect defects, damage, and safety hazards on the railway tracks. They are used by railway maintenance and inspection teams to improve the efficiency and accuracy of inspections, as well as to reduce the risk of accidents and downtime on the tracks.</p><p>The rail-mounted inspection robot market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
