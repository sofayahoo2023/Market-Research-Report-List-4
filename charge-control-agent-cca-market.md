<p><strong>The Charge Control Agent (CCA） Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negatively Charged N Series</li><li>Positively Charged P Series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Charge Control Agents (CCA) are used in various industries to control the charging behavior of particles. N Series CCAs are negatively charged, making them suitable for applications where electrostatic repulsion is needed, such as in toners and coatings. P Series CCAs, on the other hand, are positively charged and are used in applications where electrostatic attraction is required, such as in pigments and inks. Both types of CCAs play a critical role in controlling the overall performance and stability of the final product.</p></p>
