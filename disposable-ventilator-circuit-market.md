<p><p>Disposable ventilator circuits are available in three main types based on the number of water trappers included. Circuits without water trappers are basic and cost-effective options that do not include any moisture management component. Circuits with one water trapper feature a single moisture collection point, while circuits with two water trappers have dual trapping systems for improved humidity control. Healthcare providers can choose the appropriate circuit type based on their specific needs for efficient ventilation and patient comfort.</p></p>
