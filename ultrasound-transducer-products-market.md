<p><p>Ultrasound transducer products market offers various types catering to different imaging needs. Linear transducers are ideal for superficial imaging, while convex transducers provide a wider field of view for abdominal imaging. Phased array transducers are suitable for cardiac and vascular imaging. Endocavitary transducers are designed for intracavity imaging. Continuous Wave (CW) Doppler transducers are used for assessing blood flow. Other types include specialty transducers for specific applications such as 3D/4D imaging or contrast-enhanced imaging. Each type caters to different clinical requirements and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
