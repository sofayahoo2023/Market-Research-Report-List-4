<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal and External Circulation Ballscrews are both types of screw shafts used in various applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and machinery. Internal Circulation Ballscrews have the balls contained within the nut, providing smoother motion and higher load capacity. External Circulation Ballscrews have the balls circulating outside the nut, allowing for longer lengths and higher speeds. Both types offer high precision motion control capabilities and are chosen based on specific application requirements and design preferences.</p></p>
