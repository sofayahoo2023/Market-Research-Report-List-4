<p><ul><li>Single Channel Telemetry</li><li>Multi-Channel Telemetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital telemetry systems are commonly categorized into two types: single channel telemetry and multi-channel telemetry. Single channel telemetry involves the transmission of data from a single source to a single receiver, while multi-channel telemetry allows for the simultaneous transmission of data from multiple sources to multiple receivers. Single channel telemetry systems are often used in simpler applications, while multi-channel telemetry systems are preferred for more complex data collection and analysis in industries such as healthcare, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
