<p><p>Antimicrobial foam dressings are available in two main types: adhesive foam dressings and non-adhesive foam dressings. Adhesive foam dressings have a sticky backing that adheres to the skin, providing a secure and waterproof barrier. They are ideal for wounds with moderate to heavy exudate. Non-adhesive foam dressings are designed to be held in place with tape or a secondary dressing. They are suitable for wounds with minimal to moderate exudate and are more gentle on the skin during removal.</p></p>
