<p><p>Veterinary endoscopy equipment is categorized into different types based on their flexibility and design. Flexible endoscopy equipment consists of a flexible insertion tube which allows for examination of internal organs in animals. Rigid endoscopy equipment has a straight, rigid tube for a direct view of the internal organs. Capsule endoscopy involves the use of a small, ingestible capsule that captures images of the gastrointestinal tract. Other types of veterinary endoscopy equipment may include accessories or specialized tools for specific procedures.</p></p>
