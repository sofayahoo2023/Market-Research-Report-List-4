<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-Spark ignition coils are designed to generate a single spark per ignition cycle, providing a reliable and consistent spark for engine combustion. On the other hand, multi-spark ignition coils are capable of generating multiple sparks per ignition cycle, resulting in improved ignition efficiency and combustion. Single-spark ignition coils are commonly found in older vehicles, while multi-spark ignition coils are more prevalent in modern vehicles due to their ability to enhance engine performance and fuel efficiency. Both types of ignition coils play a crucial role in ensuring smooth engine operation.</p></p>
