<p><p>Pediatric stethoscopes are available in two main types: Dual Head and Single Head. Dual Head stethoscopes have two separate heads, one for auscultating high frequencies and the other for low frequencies, providing more accurate and detailed sound representation. Single Head stethoscopes, on the other hand, have only one chest piece for listening to both high and low frequencies. Both types are commonly used by healthcare professionals when examining pediatric patients for heart, lung, and other internal organ sounds.</p></p>
