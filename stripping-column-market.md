<p><p>Stripping columns are used in chemical processing to separate different components in a mixture based on their volatility. Packed stripping columns utilize packing materials to provide a larger surface area for the vapor-liquid contact, while tray stripping columns use trays or plates to separate the components. Both types of columns are key components in industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and petrochemicals. The choice between packed and tray stripping columns depends on factors such as the desired separation efficiency, pressure drop, and cost considerations.</p></p>
