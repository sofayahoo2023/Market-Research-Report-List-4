<p><p>Melting furnaces are used in foundries and metalworking industries to melt metals for casting and molding processes. The Cupola Furnace is a traditional type that burns coke or coal to generate heat. The Induction Furnace uses electromagnetic induction to heat and melt metals. The Open Hearth Furnace is a large, external combustion furnace that can produce high-quality steel. Each type of furnace has its own advantages and is used based on factors like cost, efficiency, and type of metal being melted.</p></p>
