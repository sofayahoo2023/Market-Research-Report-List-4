<p><p>The food starch market consists of four types: Native Starch, Modified Starch, Starch Derivatives, and Starch Sweeteners. Native Starch is extracted directly from plants and is used for thickening and stabilization. Modified Starch is treated to improve its functionality in different food applications. Starch Derivatives are chemically modified starches with specific properties. Starch Sweeteners are sugars derived from starch through hydrolysis or enzyme conversion. Each type of starch offers unique benefits and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
