Secondary packaging equipment is used for grouping and bundling primary packages, like cartoning machines. Labeling & serialization equipment is used to apply labels and barcodes for tracking and authentication purposes. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of pharmaceutical packaging processes.</p></p>
