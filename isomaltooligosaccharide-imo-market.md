<p><p>Isomaltooligosaccharide (IMO) is a type of prebiotic fiber derived from enzymatic conversion of starch. It can be found in different market types such as Isomaltose, Panose, and Isomalt Trisaccharide. Isomaltose is a disaccharide consisting of two glucose molecules, Panose is a trisaccharide comprised of three glucose molecules, and Isomalt Trisaccharide contains a combination of glucose, maltose, and isomaltose molecules. These IMO market types are commonly used as low-calorie sweeteners and functional ingredients in various food and beverage products.</p></p>
