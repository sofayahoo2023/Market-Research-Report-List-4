<p><p>Three-phase AC gear-motors are widely used in various industries for their efficiency and reliability. The market can be categorized into two main types: synchronous and asynchronous gear-motors. Synchronous gear-motors have a fixed speed that is synchronized with the frequency of the power supply, making them ideal for applications requiring constant speed. Asynchronous gear-motors, on the other hand, have variable speeds and are more commonly used in applications where the speed requirements may vary. Both types offer unique advantages and are suited for different industrial applications.</p></p>
