There are two main types of palm scanners in the market: optical scanning and vein scanning. </p><p>Optical scanning palm scanners use light to capture an image of the patterns on the surface of the palm, while vein scanning palm scanners use infrared light to capture images of the unique vein patterns beneath the skin. Both types of palm scanners are used for reliably identifying individuals based on their unique palm characteristics.</p></p>
