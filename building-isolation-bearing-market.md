<p><p>Building Isolation Bearings are crucial components in structures to mitigate the impact of seismic activities or vibrations. There are two main types in the market - Elastomeric Bearings and Sliding Bearings. Elastomeric Bearings are made of rubber and provide flexibility and energy dissipation, while Sliding Bearings allow for movement and sliding during seismic events. Both types are designed to isolate the building from external forces, ensuring structural integrity and safety. These bearings play a crucial role in protecting buildings and their occupants from potential damage.</p></p>
