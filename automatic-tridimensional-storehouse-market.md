<p><p>Automatic tridimensional storehouses refer to various types of automated storage and retrieval systems used in warehouses. Unit load systems are designed to store large pallets or containers. Mini load systems are used for smaller items and can be more space efficient. Vertical Lift Modules (VLM) utilize vertical space to store items and retrieve them using a robotic arm. Carousels rotate to bring items to the operator. Mid load systems combine elements of unit load and mini load systems. Autostore systems use bins stored in a grid for high-density storage.</p></p>
