<p><p>Self-Organizing Network (SON) market consists of different types including Distributed SON (D-SON), Centralized SON (C-SON), and Hybrid SON. D-SON utilizes decentralized decision making to optimize network performance. C-SON, on the other hand, centralizes decision making to efficiently manage the network. Hybrid SON combines aspects of both D-SON and C-SON, offering a flexible approach to network optimization. Each market type caters to different network requirements and offers unique benefits to operators in terms of automation, efficiency, and performance optimization.</p></p>
