Amylase helps in the breakdown of starch into sugars, aiding in the fermentation process. Protease is used for breaking down proteins in fruits and vegetables, which improves flavor and texture. Cellulase breaks down cellulose in plant cell walls, allowing for better extraction of nutrients. These enzymes play key roles in food processing and agriculture.</p></p>
