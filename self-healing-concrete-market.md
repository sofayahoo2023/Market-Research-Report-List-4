<p><p>Self-healing concrete is a material that has the ability to repair cracks autonomously. There are three main types of self-healing concrete: Intrinsic Healing, which uses the intrinsic properties of the material to heal cracks; Capsule Based Healing, which involves embedding capsules containing healing agents within the concrete that are released when cracks form; and Vascular Healing, which incorporates a network of vascular tubes within the concrete through which healing agents can flow to repair cracks.
