<p><p>Shrink film wrapping machines come in two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they complete the entire wrapping process, from placing the product on the conveyor belt to sealing and cutting the film. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require more manual work, as operators need to feed the product into the machine and activate the wrapping process. Both types offer efficient packaging solutions, but fully-automatic machines are preferred for high-volume production lines due to their faster processing speeds.</p></p>
