<p><p>Medical examination tables are available in four main types: manual, hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Manual tables are operated by hand, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Hydraulic tables use fluid power to adjust table height and position, providing smooth and precise movements. Electric tables are controlled by electric motors, allowing for easy adjustment at the touch of a button. Pneumatic tables utilize air pressure to adjust the table position, offering a lightweight and versatile option. Each type of table caters to different needs and preferences in the medical field.</p></p>
