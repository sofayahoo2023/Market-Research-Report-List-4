<p><p>Large equipment seats market can be classified into two main types based on suspension systems: Mechanical Suspension Seats and Air Suspension Seats. Mechanical suspension seats use springs and shock absorbers to offer a comfortable ride for operators. On the other hand, Air suspension seats use compressed air to provide a smoother and more adjustable seating experience, reducing the impact of vibrations and shocks on the operator. Both types aim to enhance comfort, reduce fatigue, and improve overall productivity for operators of large equipment.</p></p>
