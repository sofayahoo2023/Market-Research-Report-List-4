<p><p>Laser vibrometers are devices used to measure vibrations in structures and machinery. Single point laser vibrometers focus on measuring vibrations at one specific point, providing detailed information about the vibration amplitude and frequency at that location. On the other hand, multi-point laser vibrometers can measure vibrations at multiple points simultaneously, offering a wider range of data for analyzing complex vibration patterns. Both types of laser vibrometers are essential tools in fields such as engineering, automotive, aerospace, and research.</p></p>
