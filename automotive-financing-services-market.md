<p><strong>The Automotive Financing Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straightforward Car Loan</li><li>Hire Purchase</li><li>Personal Contract Hire (Car Leasing)</li><li>Personal Contract Purchase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Straightforward car loans are the most common type of automotive financing where the borrower takes out a loan to purchase a vehicle and repays it over time with interest. Hire purchase involves making regular payments on a vehicle until the full amount is paid off, after which ownership is transferred. Personal Contract Hire, or car leasing, allows individuals to use a vehicle for a set period of time and make monthly payments without owning it. Personal Contract Purchase combines car leasing and hire purchase, giving the option to purchase the vehicle at the end of the lease period.</p></p>
