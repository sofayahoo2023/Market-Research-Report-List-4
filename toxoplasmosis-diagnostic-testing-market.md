These tests help in detecting antibodies produced by the body in response to Toxoplasma gondii infection. IFAT and MAT are used for detecting antibodies in animal hosts, while ELISA is commonly used in laboratories for human testing. Immunoglobulin tests help in determining the stage of infection and the immune response to the parasite.</p></p>
