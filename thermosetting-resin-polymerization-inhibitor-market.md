<p><p>Thermosetting resin polymerization inhibitors refer to substances that can prevent or slow down the polymerization process of thermosetting resins. There are two main types of inhibitors in the market: molecular type and stable radical type. Molecular type inhibitors work by interrupting the polymerization reaction at a molecular level, while stable radical type inhibitors act as radical scavengers to inhibit polymerization. Both types are essential in industries like coatings, adhesives, and composites where precise control over the polymerization process is crucial for product quality.</p></p>
