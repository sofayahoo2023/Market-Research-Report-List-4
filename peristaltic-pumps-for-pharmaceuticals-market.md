<p><strong>The Peristaltic Pumps for Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps for pharmaceuticals market includes two main types: roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps use rotating rollers to compress flexible tubing, creating a continuous flow of liquid. Shoe type peristaltic pumps use a shoe or wedge to compress the tubing against the pump casing, also creating a smooth flow of liquid. Both types are widely used in the pharmaceutical industry due to their gentle pumping action, accuracy, and contamination-free operation.</p></p>
