<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robotic window cleaners are available in two main market types: Vacuum Suction and Fan Adsorption. Vacuum Suction window cleaners use a powerful vacuum to create suction against the window surface, allowing them to move and clean effectively. On the other hand, Fan Adsorption window cleaners use high-speed rotating fans to create an airflow that adheres them to the window surface. Both types offer efficient and convenient solutions for cleaning windows in homes and commercial buildings.</p></p>
