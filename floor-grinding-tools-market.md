<p><p>Floor grinding tools market can be categorized into two main types: dry polishing and wet polishing. Dry polishing involves using equipment that does not require water for the process, making it more suitable for indoor applications where water may be a concern. On the other hand, wet polishing utilizes water during the grinding process to reduce dust and keep the tools cool, making it ideal for outdoor projects or areas where dust control is a priority. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
