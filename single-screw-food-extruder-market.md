<p><p>Single screw food extruders are classified into two main types based on the process involved: cold extrusion and hot extrusion. Cold extrusion food extruders operate at lower temperatures, making them suitable for processing heat-sensitive ingredients like vitamins and enzymes. On the other hand, hot extrusion food extruders operate at higher temperatures and are ideal for processing starches, proteins, and fibers. Both types of extruders are widely used in the food industry for producing a variety of products such as snacks, breakfast cereals, and pet foods.</p></p>
