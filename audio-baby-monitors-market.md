<p><ul><li>Wired Audio Baby Monitors</li><li>Wireless Audio Baby Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wired Audio Baby Monitors require a physical connection between the baby unit and parent unit through cords or cables. They offer a reliable and secure connection but limited mobility. On the other hand, Wireless Audio Baby Monitors operate through a radio frequency to transmit sounds from the baby's room to the parent unit. They provide more flexibility and freedom of movement, but may be prone to interference. Both types of monitors are popular choices for parents looking to keep an ear on their baby's wellbeing.</p></p>
