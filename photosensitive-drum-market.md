<p><ul><li>Integrated Photosensitive Drum</li><li>Non-Integrated Photosensitive Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Photosensitive Drum refers to a type of drum that is built-in and cannot be removed or replaced separately from the printer. This type of drum is commonly found in all-in-one printers and multifunction devices. On the other hand, Non-Integrated Photosensitive Drum refers to a drum unit that can be easily removed and replaced independently from the printer. This type of drum is typically used in laser printers where the drum needs to be replaced separately from the toner cartridge.</p></p>
