<p><ul><li>Water Tube Type</li><li>Smoke Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine auxiliary boilers are available in two main types: water tube type and smoke tube type. Water tube boilers use water-filled tubes to transfer heat and produce steam, while smoke tube boilers utilize hot gases from combustion to heat the water and generate steam. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, with water tube boilers typically being more efficient and compact, while smoke tube boilers are generally simpler in design and easier to maintain. The choice between these types depends on specific requirements and preferences of the marine industry.</p></p>
