<p><ul><li>Bladder</li><li>Piston</li><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Accumulator markets are classified based on the type of technology used. Bladder accumulators have a flexible bladder separating the gas and fluid, allowing for high-pressure operation. Piston accumulators use a sliding piston to separate the gas and fluid, providing efficient energy storage. Diaphragm accumulators employ a flexible diaphragm to separate the gas and fluid, ideal for high-pressure applications. Other market types may include hybrid accumulators or specialized designs catering to specific industrial needs. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
