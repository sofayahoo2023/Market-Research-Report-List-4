<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Intermittent</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial frying equipment market can be classified into three types: continuous, intermittent, and other markets. Continuous frying equipment is designed for high-volume production with a continuous flow of product through the fryer. Intermittent frying equipment is more suitable for smaller batches and allows for product loading and unloading between frying cycles. The other market encompasses various specialized frying equipment types that may not fit into the continuous or intermittent categories, such as batch fryers or specialized frying systems for specific products.</p></p>
